{% import "bootstrap/wtf.html" as wtf %} Child Labor

More than 200 million children in the world today are not playing outside or gaining an education. Intead, they are being forced to produce the world's most consumed goods.

Many of the countries with the highest rates of child labor have poor to absolutely no legal reinforcement.
And most countries partake in commercial sexual exploitation.

Below is a list of almost 100 countries detailing the child labor percentage, the type of industries they are in, goods produced by these children and ways you can help.

List of Countries Affected by Child Labor

This app will highlight the number of countries with the highest rates of child labor and hopefully shed light to the urgent need to stop it. It will presenst the industries as well as the goods that are made in that country. Additionally, most countries will give a child labor free alternative (popularly known as Fair Trade) to either the goods listed or in general.

We all can make a difference.
